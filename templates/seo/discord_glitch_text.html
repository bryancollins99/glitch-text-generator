Discord Text Formatting Guide

In addition to glitch effects, Discord supports various text formatting options:

  • Bold: Surround text with double asterisks (**bold**)
  • Italic: Surround text with single asterisks (*italic*)
  • Underline: Surround text with double underscores (__underline__)
  • Strikethrough: Surround text with double tildes (~~strikethrough~~)
  • Code blocks: Surround text with backticks (`code`)

You can combine these formatting options with your glitched text for even more unique effects!

Discord Nitro and Special Characters

While Nitro doesn't directly affect your ability to use glitched text, it complements well with a unique glitched username for maximum customization.
