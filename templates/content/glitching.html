What is Glitching?

Glitching is the practice of intentionally introducing errors, corruption, or malfunctions into digital systems to create artistic effects and aesthetic experiences.

Glitching Techniques

  • Data manipulation and file corruption
  • Hardware circuit bending
  • Software exploit and bug abuse
  • Signal interference and noise
  • Compression artifact creation

Tools for Glitching

  • Text editors for databending
  • Image manipulation software
  • Audio editing applications
  • Programming environments
  • Hardware modification tools

Creative Applications

  • Digital art and installations
  • Music production and sound design
  • Video art and experimental film
  • Interactive media and games
  • Social media content creation
