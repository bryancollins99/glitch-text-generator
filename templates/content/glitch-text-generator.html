Tips for Effective Glitch Text Usage

Readability Balance: Maintain text legibility while achieving desired corruption effects. Heavily distorted text works for artistic purposes but may frustrate readers in functional contexts.

Platform Testing: Verify glitch text displays correctly across target platforms. Some social networks filter or remove certain Unicode characters, potentially breaking effects.

Contrast Considerations: Ensure corrupted text maintains sufficient contrast against backgrounds. Additional corruption marks can reduce readability in low-contrast situations.

Brand Consistency: Develop signature glitch styles that align with overall brand aesthetics. Consistent corruption patterns help establish recognizable visual identity.

Accessibility Awareness: Provide alt-text or plain text alternatives for heavily corrupted content to ensure accessibility for screen readers and users with visual impairments.
