About Small Caps

Small caps are uppercase letters that are sized to match the x-height of lowercase letters. They're commonly used in:

  • Typography: Headers, subheadings, and emphasis
  • Branding: Logos and company names
  • Academic: Citations and references
  • Design: Creating visual hierarchy
  • Legal: Terms and conditions, contracts
