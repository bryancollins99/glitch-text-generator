About Italic Text

Italic text creates emphasis and elegance through slanted letterforms. Commonly used for:

  • Emphasis: Highlighting important words and phrases
  • Quotes: Book titles, foreign words, and citations
  • Typography: Creating visual hierarchy and flow
  • Design: Headers, captions, and stylistic elements
  • Social Media: Stylish posts and attention-grabbing text
  • Academic: Scientific names and technical terms
